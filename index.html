The Creator

About Me

Alexandra Curry is a storyteller first and a designer second, though the two have never really been separate. Growing up with a head full of characters and worlds that needed somewhere to go, collaborative fiction became the natural home for both — a space where a single choice ripples outward and changes everything.

With a background in Immersive Media & Games (IMG) from Northern Arizona University, and years of collaborative roleplay shaping the way they think about narrative and player agency, they bring a writer's instinct to every design problem: who is this for, what will they feel, and what happens next? Their work spans high fantasy world-building, emotionally grounded character drama, and systems that reward the kind of small, human decisions that butterfly effects are made of.

Currently looking for opportunities in game design, narrative design, or world-building roles. Open to collaborations, freelance, and full-time positions.

If you're building something that needs a collaborator who genuinely cares about the people who'll experience it, let's talk.
